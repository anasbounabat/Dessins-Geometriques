Bio

I'm Anas Bounabat, a student at IIM Paris La Défense studying digital creation and technology.

On Generative Art

Generative art is about building systems that make decisions you didn't explicitly program. You write the rules, the algorithm writes the image.

What fascinates me is that the same code can produce infinite outputs — and within those constraints, there's still a signature, a style, a hand.
